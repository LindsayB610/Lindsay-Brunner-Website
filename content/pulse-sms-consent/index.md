Pulse is a Brunner Creative-operated reminder tool that can send low-volume SMS reminders configured by the recipient or account owner.

## How SMS Opt-In Works

Pulse SMS reminders are enabled only when a person intentionally configures Pulse for their own reminders or asks Brunner Creative to configure Pulse reminders for a phone number they control.

Reviewers can view the opt-in screen here: [Pulse SMS opt-in flow screenshot](/images/pulse-sms-opt-in-flow.png).

![Pulse SMS opt-in flow screenshot showing an unchecked SMS consent checkbox](/images/pulse-sms-opt-in-flow.png)

Before SMS reminders are enabled, the recipient is told that:

- messages are recurring operational reminder texts from Brunner Creative Pulse;
- message frequency varies based on the reminders configured;
- message and data rates may apply;
- they can reply **STOP** to opt out;
- they can reply **HELP** for help;
- SMS consent is not a condition of purchasing goods or services;
- SMS consent is not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

Pulse is not used for marketing or promotional SMS.

## What Messages Say

Pulse messages are short operational reminders, such as:

> Pulse reminder: Your recurring reminder is due now. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help.

> Pulse reminder: Your weekly reminder is due now. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help.

## Opt-Out And Help

Reply **STOP** to opt out of Pulse SMS reminders. Reply **HELP** for help.

You can also contact [lb@lindsaybrunner.com](mailto:lb@lindsaybrunner.com) with questions about Pulse SMS reminders.
